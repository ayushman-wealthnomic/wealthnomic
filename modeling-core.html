INITIATIVE

Modeling Core

The high-performance C++/CUDA core for quantitative modeling and stochastic calculus. Based on QuantLib, providing accelerated derivatives pricing and risk modeling.

The Thesis

Precision at scale requires local execution anyway. Cloud-based LLMs cannot handle the heavy lifting of real-time volatility surface calibration or million-path Monte Carlo simulations.

Modeling Core provides the "math brain" of the foundation. By integrating with QuantLib and adding a custom CUDA acceleration layer, we allow researchers to price exotics and calculate sensitivities (Greeks) at institutional speeds without proprietary terminals.

dSt = μStdt + σStdWt // Core Stochastic Evolution

Implemented Models

Heston Model

Captures the volatility smile via stochastic variance evolution. Integral-based semi-analytical pricing.

SABR Surface

The industry standard for interest rate and FX models. Fast calibration to market skews.

Dupire Local Vol

Consistent pricing across all strikes and maturities. Full surface interpolation tools.

Model Calibration

Automated calibration of parameters to market data using Levenberg-Marquardt optimizers.

Performance Example (Python)

# Calibrate a Heston model to market volatilies from quantcore import HestonModel, Calibrator model = HestonModel(spot=100.0, rate=0.05) calibrator = Calibrator(market_data="options_snapshot.csv") params = calibrator.fit(model, method="cuda_accelerated") print(f"Calibrated Kappa: {params.kappa:.4f}")
