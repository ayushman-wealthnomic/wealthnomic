Methods & Explainability

Research-grade methods for transparent and explainable AI. Providing verifiable, interpretable insights into every model decision and automated signal.

The Thesis

Trust is non-negotiable in institutional finance. Black-box AI that cannot explain *why* it made a trade is a regulatory liability. As AI-related financial incidents increase, the need for transparency becomes paramount.

Methods & Explainability ensures that your AI is both rigorous and interpretable. We integrate leading XAI techniques like SHAP and LIME with comprehensive bias detection and blockchain-based audit logs to provide full transparency into your modeling decisions.

Explainability & Compliance

SHAP LIME Hyperledger Model Cards Bias Detection SEC Reporting

Methods & Explainability provides:

  • Local Explanations: Precisely which features (e.g., RSI, Sentiment, Volume) triggered a specific trade.
  • Global Interpretation: Understanding the overall strategy bias across a 12-month period.
  • Compliance Reports: One-click generation of MiFID II / SEC-ready documentation.
  • Immutable Logs: Every decision signed and hashed onto a private ledger.

# Log a decision with SHAP values from audittrail import AuditClient, Explainer log = AuditClient(ledger="hyperledger://prod-01") explainer = Explainer(model=trading_model) shap_values = explainer.explain(input_data) log.record_decision( action="BUY", ticker="AAPL", reasoning=shap_values, compliance_id="SEC-2025-A99" )
