Firm Overview
About Wealthnomic

Wealthnomic is a specialist asset manager focused exclusively on systematic, rules-based investment strategies across liquid global markets. Our mandate is to provide investors with transparent, repeatable processes that seek to harness risk premia, market inefficiencies and structural dislocations over a full cycle.

The firm is built on three core pillars: disciplined research, robust risk management and institutional-grade operations. We combine quantitative models with pragmatic portfolio construction, prioritizing capital preservation while targeting attractive risk-adjusted returns.

Wealthnomic strategies are designed to complement traditional equity and fixed-income portfolios by emphasizing:

  • Diversified exposures across asset classes, regions and time horizons.
  • Systematic decision-making to reduce behavioral and discretionary biases.
  • Continuous model evaluation, stress testing and scenario analysis.
  • Operational infrastructure aligned with institutional best practices.

Investment Approach
Strategy Architecture

The Wealthnomic platform is organized into modular strategy sleeves, allowing allocators to access diversified exposures or to construct bespoke mandates. The following components are illustrative only and describe generic strategy types typically employed by systematic managers.

Sleeve I
Systematic Macro

Quantitative models trading major futures and FX markets, seeking to capture medium-term trends and macro risk premia. Positions are sized with volatility and liquidity constraints designed to avoid undue concentration.

Sleeve II
Equity Market-Neutral

Long/short portfolios built from diversified factor and stock-selection signals. The objective is to neutralize broad market beta while targeting idiosyncratic alpha and style premia such as value, quality and momentum.

Sleeve III
Alternative Carry & Income

Systematic carry strategies across rates, FX and volatility markets, combined with prudent hedging and strict drawdown controls. Emphasis is placed on liquidity, diversification and scenario testing.

Platform
Research & Governance

An ongoing research process governs model development, data quality and implementation. Investment guidelines, risk thresholds and operational protocols are overseen by independent governance functions.

Risk & Governance
Capital Preservation First

Risk management is embedded in every stage of the Wealthnomic investment process. Portfolios are constructed using explicit risk budgets, scenario analysis and ongoing monitoring of exposures, liquidity and concentration.

Key portfolio-level risk tools typically include:

  • Volatility and drawdown constraints at fund and sleeve level.
  • Position and exposure limits by asset class, instrument and region.
  • Liquidity and financing metrics based on conservative assumptions.
  • Historical, hypothetical and forward-looking scenario analysis.

Governance and operational controls are designed to align interests and support institutional due diligence:

  • Segregation of duties between portfolio management, risk and operations.
  • Independent service providers for administration, audit and custody.
  • Documented policies covering valuation, trade approvals and escalation.
  • Regular reporting to investors on performance, exposures and risk metrics.

Investing in hedge funds involves risk, including the possible loss of the entire amount invested. Past performance is not indicative of future results.
